With an assumed reading rate of four books per year.

The overwhelming majority of our time spent with our parents occurs when we are children. In most cases, childhood involves daily trips home to one or more parents. After adulthood, for those who move to different cities, the rate drops from daily in-person experiences to just a few visits a year.

We are here for a limited period of time. Enjoy life, let your loved ones know how much you appreciate them, and do your part to leave the world a better place than you found it.
